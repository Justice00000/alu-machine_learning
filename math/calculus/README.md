# Calculus
=======
# Calculus and Mathematical Series Overview

## Introduction

This repository contains notes, explanations, and resources on fundamental concepts in calculus and mathematical series, with a focus on their applications in machine learning (ML). The topics covered range from sigma and pi notation to integration, derivatives, and advanced topics such as Bernoulli numbers and polynomials.

---

## Topics Covered

### 1. **Mathematical Notations**
   - **Sigma Notation (Σ)**: Explains summation, commonly used for denoting sums in series.
     - Starting at 0:32.
   - **Product Notation (Π)**: Covers product notation for multiplying sequences.
     - Up to 0:20.
   - **Sigma and Pi Notation**: A comparative overview of both notations in mathematical series.

### 2. **Series and Sums**
   - **What is a Series?**: Defines mathematical series and the difference between finite and infinite series.
   - **Mathematical Series**: Introduction to different types of series in mathematics, particularly in sums of powers.
   - **List of Mathematical Series**: Focuses on common series, including sums of powers.

### 3. **Bernoulli Numbers and Polynomials**
   - **Bernoulli Numbers (Bn)**: A deep dive into Bernoulli numbers and their significance in series expansions.
   - **Bernoulli Polynomials (Bn(x))**: Introduces Bernoulli polynomials and their properties.

### 4. **Derivatives**
   - **What is a Derivative?**: A general introduction to the concept of derivatives in calculus.
   - **Calculus for Machine Learning**: Highlights the importance of calculus, particularly derivatives, in machine learning.
   - **1 of 2: Seeing the Big Picture**: An overview of why understanding derivatives is crucial.
   - **2 of 2: First Principles**: Introduces the concept of derivative calculation from first principles.
   - **1 of 2: Finding the Derivative**: Practical steps for finding the derivative of a function.
   - **2 of 2: What Do We Discover?**: Insights gained from the derivative.
   - **Deriving a Rule for Differentiating Powers of x**: A step-by-step derivation of the power rule for differentiation.
   - **1 of 3: Introducing a Substitution**: Using substitution to simplify derivatives.
   - **2 of 3: Combining Derivatives**: Advanced techniques for combining multiple derivatives.
   - **How to Understand Derivatives**: Focuses on important rules in differentiation:
     - Product Rule
     - Power Rule
     - Chain Rule
   - **Product Rule**: An in-depth look at the product rule for differentiation.
   - **Common Derivatives and Integrals**: A reference guide to the most commonly used derivatives and integrals.
   - **Partial Derivatives**: Introduction to partial derivatives and their applications.
     - **Introduction to Partial Derivatives**
     - **How to Solve Partial Derivatives?**

### 5. **Integrals**
   - **What is an Integral?**: Introduction to the concept of integrals and their importance in calculus.
   - **Integration and the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus**: Explanation of how integration and differentiation are connected.
   - **Introduction to Integration**: Covers basic concepts of integration.
   - **Indefinite Integral**: Basic integration rules, problems, and formulas, including trigonometric functions.
   - **Definite Integral**: Explanation of definite integrals, their applications, and examples.
   - **Multiple Integrals**: Advanced integration techniques.
     - **Double Integral 1**
     - **Double Integral 2**

## How to Use This Repository

- Start with **Mathematical Notations** for foundational understanding.
- Move on to **Derivatives** and **Integrals** for a deeper dive into calculus concepts.
- Use the **Bernoulli Numbers and Polynomials** section for advanced series-related studies.
- Review **Calculus for Machine Learning** to understand the direct application of these mathematical concepts in machine learning.
